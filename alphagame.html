Description

AlphaGame aims to use machine learning to play simple games. This project utilizes reinforcement learning algorithms as cores of AI to play simple games. In the paper, we first discuss how the theories of Deep Q-Learning and genetic algorithm relate to our design choices and implementation. Then, we explain methods which include our model architectures and real-time data collection methods, followed by a discussion of our results. We evaluate the performance of two AI on three games, Snake, Pong, and Flappy Bird.
