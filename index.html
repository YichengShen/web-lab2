Projects
Eagles Peer Evaluation System

Spring 2020

A software that allows professors to create peer evaluations and students to evaluate each other. Professors can easily manage courses, teams, and created peer evaluations. Students can fill out peer evaluations. Professors will see aggregated results once students finish their peer evaluations. Students will see their score and comments once professors release the results.
